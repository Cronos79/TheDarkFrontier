Core Principles
Gameplay First

The player should immediately recognize every object.

Silhouette First

Silhouette is more important than texture.

Players identify shape before detail.

Chunky Geometry

Slight exaggeration is encouraged.

Examples

Large beams

Thick roofs

Oversized wagon wheels

Heavy wooden supports

Readability

Everything should be recognizable from the city-builder camera.

If details disappear,

make them larger.

The 10 Meter Rule

Imagine standing ten meters away.

Ask

Can I still recognize this object?

If not,

improve the silhouette.
