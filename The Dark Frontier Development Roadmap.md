One philosophy I'd like us to stick to

I think we should adopt a design rule that will keep The Dark Frontier feeling fresh:

Progress should come from three different sources:

Growth ‚Äî expanding your settlement (Town Hall and infrastructure).
Mastery ‚Äî improving by doing and gaining experience.
Discovery ‚Äî exploring the world and uncovering forgotten knowledge.

That gives players multiple paths to progress. Two settlements of the same size might have completely different capabilities because one became expert foresters, while the other uncovered ancient magic or learned to work with dragons. I think that variety will give The Dark Frontier its own identity rather than feeling like another city builder with a linear tech tree
